Animated Lyric Video - “The Beach” by Giveon

A type centric lyric video I made to Giveon's song "The Beach". Captruring the essence of mystery, fear, security, and leaps of faith, all under the umbrella of an infatuation for another person.

Skills

Motion Graphics, Storytelling

Draft

This version had a little more graphical imagery. Especially the building that the words jump off of symbolizing a leap of faith and the words "scared" and "care" that are the foundation for the actions described in the song.

Final

Cleaned up the animations and went with a type centric approach to focus in on the words of the song.
