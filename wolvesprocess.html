Book Cover Creation Process (Video)

I picked three episodes from the “This Is Love” podcast and turned them into a trilogy of book covers. Starting with “The Wolves”, a story about the dynamic relationships of wolves at the Yellowstone National Park with a focus on two wolves. The challenges for these two wolves to live up to the expectations of their packs conflicting with their bond. This video shows my process for the first book cover of the trilogy.
